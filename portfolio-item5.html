Grade Predictor Linear Regression

The ML approach to student performance evaluation

Utilizing linear regression, the model takes two grades, the study time, number of failures, and absences of a high school student, and attempts to predict his/her final grade. The training dataset of students' information was collected from the UCI (University of California, Irvine) machine learning repository, an archive containing 557 datasets.

Program: Python

Libraries utilized: pandas, numpy, sklearn, matplotlib, pickle

Accuracy achieved: 92%
