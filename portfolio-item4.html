Cancer Assessment Support Vector Machines

The ML approach to breast cancer assessment

Utilizing the algorithm, Support Vector Machine, The model takes patients' breast cancer characteristics, such as mean radius, mean texture, mean perimeter, mean area, etc. to determine if the cancer is malignant or benign.

Program: Python

Libraries utilized: sklearn

Accuracy achieved: 93.86%
