Vehicle Evaluation K-Nearest Neighors

The ML approach to product evaluation

Utilizing the algorithm, K-Nearest Neighbors, the model takes the overall buying price, maintenance cost, number of doors, capacity of number of persons, size of the luggage boot, and safety level of a specific vehicle, and attempts to predict the vehicle's "acceptability". Acceptability (cls column in car.data) was determined by "... a simple hierarchical decision model originally developed for the demonstration of DEX, M. Bohanec, V. Rajkovic: Expert system for decision making. (Sistemica 1(1), pp. 145-157, 1990.)."

Program: Python

Libraries utilized: pandas, numpy, sklearn

Accuracy achieved: 94.22%
